'Meaningful IO' City University of Hong Kong research study account deletion

Version 1.0.0, 12/05/2023

As mentioned in the participation consent information, you may request to withdraw from the study and delete all your associated data at any time.

To withdraw, simply send an email from the email address you used to register your Transcrobes account to Anton Melser (anton@transcrob.es) and request removal. You data will be completely removed from the database as soon as possible.
